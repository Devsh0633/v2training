Questions About SimpliTrain and LMS for Training Companies

An LMS for training companies is built for organisations that sell and deliver training to paying clients — not for internal employee training. It includes multi-tenant client portals, eCommerce, a built-in training scheduler (TMS), and flat-rate pricing that doesn't scale with learner volume. SimpliTrain combines LMS, TMS, and LXP in one platform for this exact use case.
Off-the-shelf LMS platforms charge per learner, support only one organisation, and have no training scheduling or course storefront. SimpliTrain uses flat-rate per-admin pricing, supports unlimited client tenants, includes a built-in TMS for ILT and vILT, and has native eCommerce — purpose-built for training companies with multiple paying clients.
An LMS (Learning Management System) manages course delivery and learner tracking. A TMS (Training Management System) handles instructor-led training logistics — scheduling, venues, waitlists. An LXP (Learning Experience Platform) personalises the learner experience with recommendations and social learning. SimpliTrain combines all three in a single subscription, eliminating the need for separate tools.
